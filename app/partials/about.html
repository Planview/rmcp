About the Research

Research Methodology, Demographics and Participant Profile

Seeking to Understand the Resource Dilemma

Organization leaders responsible for people resources in various industries have perennially expressed challenges around maximizing the efficiency of their people resources. In particular, they have widely agreed that one of the top pain points is that of over-committing resources and the business implications that persist as a result. The research study was commissioned by Planview to identify and benchmark the state of resource management and capacity planning at larger organizations.

Methodology & Respondent Profile

In May 2014, Appleseed Partners and Dig Market Research conducted an online survey of 480 resource leaders from around the globe. The online survey was preceded by qualitative phone interviews for the development of the survey. The resource leaders included executives, directors, and managers with responsibility for the resource management and/or capacity planning process at their organization.
